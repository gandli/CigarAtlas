**Prepared by:** Market Analyst · User Researcher · Product Manager

---

## 1. Executive Summary

The Chinese cigar market continues to expand, with clear trends toward premiumization, circle-based social consumption, and digital behavior shifts. Existing cigar communities validate demand but largely remain at a “forum/wiki 1.0” stage.

Across multiple research inputs, user pain points converge into three primary anxieties:

1. **Knowledge Anxiety** — onboarding is difficult and fragmented.
2. **Social Anxiety** — communities are scattered across private groups and disconnected channels.
3. **Trust & Care Anxiety** — counterfeit risk, opaque channels, and complex storage/maintenance.

CigarAtlas should position itself as a **digital tasting and trust infrastructure for cigar enthusiasts**, not merely another content forum.

Recommended MVP focus:
- Structured onboarding and learning
- Cigar journal and personal preference tracking
- Core community interactions

## 2. Unified Market View

### 2.1 Industry Trends

- China remains one of the fastest-growing cigar consumption markets.
- Consumption is shifting from identity signaling to **experience + culture + social rituals**.
- Core demand concentrates in Tier-1 and New Tier-1 cities, while lower-tier markets show latent potential.
- Domestic cigar brands are rising, creating demand for content-based education and trust-building.

### 2.2 Core User Segments

- **Primary users:** Men aged 30–50, middle/high income, entrepreneurs/executives/professionals.
- **Growth users:** Younger urban professionals and beginner female consumers.
- **Core motivations:**
  1. Social and circle identity
  2. Ritualized self-reward and lifestyle enjoyment
  3. Collection and advanced tasting progression

---

## 3. Consolidated User Pain Points

### 3.1 Knowledge Gaps

- New users lack a structured learning path.
- Product and tasting information is fragmented and difficult to verify.
- Advanced users need deeper, longitudinal, data-backed tasting references.

### 3.2 Social Fragmentation

- Discussions are split across WeChat groups, forums, short-video platforms, and private circles.
- Offline activities are hard to discover and often high-threshold.
- Lack of cross-circle discovery and identity continuity.

### 3.3 Trust & Maintenance Risks

- Counterfeit concern is persistent for high-value purchases.
- Channel transparency is low; reliability is uncertain.
- Storage requires knowledge and discipline (temperature/humidity), with high loss risk if mishandled.

---

## 4. Competitive Landscape & Opportunity

### 4.1 Existing Product Patterns

Most current products offer:
- basic community posting
- lightweight encyclopedia content
- occasional activity aggregation

Main limitations:
- no structured tasting record system
- weak trust/risk infrastructure
- minimal data utility for personalization
- limited integration with maintenance workflows and device ecosystems

### 4.2 Strategic White Space

CigarAtlas can differentiate via:
- **Tooling:** Cigar journal, flavor profile, maintenance reminders
- **Trust layer:** compliant offline channel directory, verification checklist workflow
- **Social layer:** circle-based interactions, activity system, contribution/credibility progression

---

## 5. Product Positioning & Compliance Boundary

### Positioning

**CigarAtlas = A digital tasting and trust community for cigar enthusiasts.**

### Compliance Boundary (Mandatory)

- The app does **not** provide online tobacco sales.
- The app provides: knowledge content, community interaction, compliant channel navigation, risk alerts, and activity organization.

## 6. Feature Roadmap (MVP → V2)

### P0 (MVP)

1. **Learning Camp** (structured onboarding for beginners)
2. **Cigar Journal** (notes, flavor tags, ratings, history)
3. **Core Community** (posts, Q&A, activity posts)

### P1 (Enhancement)

4. **Maintenance Assistant** (manual logging + rule-based reminders)
5. **Compliant Channel Navigation** (store directory, credentials tags, user feedback)

### P2 (Future)

6. AI-assisted authenticity guidance
7. Live tasting events
8. IoT humidor API integration
9. Transaction-related capabilities (only after strict legal and risk validation)
